Roast Pork

Description

Maple-glazed pork loin roast is great for Christmas. You'll love this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds boneless pork loin roast
  • 1 cup real maple syrup
  • ¼ cup prepared Dijon-style mustard
  • 2 ½ tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons soy sauce
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Cooking process

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    Place pork roast into a shallow roasting pan.
  2. Stir maple syrup, mustard, vinegar, soy sauce, salt,
    and pepper together in a small bowl;
    spread evenly over pork roast.
